Backdrop for FOS

Today for every business big or small, having a virtual presence is as important as having a registered office / shop (premise) in the real world. I see only 2 ways every business can do it either via website or having social media presence.

Having a website is like having an shop/ office in some remote corner of the city wherein business has to constantly, engage in marketing, seo and other activities to attract users/clients.

While having a social media presence is like having a leased establishment in a mall, wherein there are ample users/clients, but the business can never own the space, data, content, community and can be silenced, banned, removed, etc. Thus soley relying on the mercy of the owners/administrators. What do we plan to build?

FOS - Fediverse on Steroids!!!

With FOS we envision a future where businesses can enjoy the best of both worlds. FOS is an opensource solution that offers all levels of business from small to big, from individuals to firms, complete control over their virtual space, just like a website, along with networking capabilities of social media platform.

FOS helps you to break free from the clutches of centralised systems and embrace collaborative power of community-driven business and tech solutions built for the community era !

Introducing FOS - Fediverse on steroids

Omnichannel & Seamless Workflow:

  • Omnichannel Seamless Integration: Connect your existing social media and messaging platforms with ease. No more juggling multiple platforms!
  • Advanced Search & Filtering: Find exactly what you're looking for with powerful search functionalities and customizable filters integrated with AI.

Empowering Users:

  • Real-time Collaboration: Connect and collaborate with other users in real-time for a more efficient and streamlined workflow.
  • Data Ownership & Control: Today tech giants like Google, meta, Microsoft etc know about us, what we do, where we eat, where we live, travel and almost everything. The above data combined with the new breakthroughs in AI/ LLM space provides more power to these companies and adds more uncertainties for small businesses, and individuals. FOS strives to bring back balance. With FOS everyone can maintain complete control over their data within the secure decentralised network. Your data, your choice!

True Freedom:

  • Platform-agnostic: Experience performance similar to that of native apps across any platform be it windows, mac, linux, or andriod.
  • Device-agnostic: Freedom to choose his own device be it raspberry pi, local machine or high end servers, user can choose his own device.

Decentralized Power:

  • Decentralized & Serverless: Experience a real estate ecosystem independent of centralized control, built on a serverless infrastructure for ultimate scalability. Your data, your rules!
  • Blockchain-powered Security: Ensure the highest level of data security and transparency for your business through blockchain technology.

Collaborative Knowledge:

  • Community-driven Knowledge Base: Tap into a wealth of real estate knowledge built and maintained by the community itself. Learn from the collective expertise!
  • SLM/LLM by the Community: Inspired by Project Petals (https://github.com/bigscience-workshop/petals), FOS empowers the community to create and manage its own AI assistants (SLM/LLM) for a truly collaborative experience.
